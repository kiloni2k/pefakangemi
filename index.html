About

THE PENTECOSTAL EVANGELISTIC FELLOWSHIP OF AFRICA (P.E.F.A.) DEVELOPED FROM THE PARTNERSHIP AND MERGER OF CHURCHES THAT HAD BEEN FOUNDED BY ELIM FELLOWSHIP AND THE INTERNATIONAL PENTECOSTAL ASSEMBLIES. THESE TWO MISSIONARY BODIES AGREED TO UNITE THEIR CHURCHES TO FORM THE PENTECOSTAL EVANGELISTIC FELLOWSHIP OF AFRICA.

P.E.F.A. in essence means: a vibrant Pentecostal Church where the ministry gifts of the Holy Spirit are in operation in the lives of the church members. It is an Evangelist Church, envisioned to preach the Full Gospel of Jesus Christ - salvation, healing and baptism of the Holy Spirit. It is also a church where believers fellowship together and recognize themselves as the very body of Jesus Christ. Last but not least, P.E.F.A. is a Continental Church that has spread its wings across the Continent of Africa. As this history is being written, P.E.F.A. Church has spread its wings to other countries of the world.

  • Mission: To build and expand the kingdom of God by preaching and teaching the Wholistic Gospel of Jesus Christ for the transformation and fulfillment of lives.
  • Vision: To be the church tha knows christ and makes Him Known.
  • Motto: To Know Christ and Make Him Known.

PEFA Kangemi A is located along Thiongo Road near kangemi Primary. We definitely have Pentecostal and Evangelical beginnings, despite the fact that we are a COMMUNITY church with a non-denominational spirit and welcome people from all religion backgrounds to join us in worshiping Christ. We are a part of the ELIM Churches in the US associated Pentecostal Evangelical Fellowship of Africa (PEFA). In order to further the Kingdom of God, we also collaborate with a variety of churches and organizations. MEN

Men Week, Third week of June every year

This expanding ministry was created to provide a setting where men can sharpen one another via interpersonal connections. The goal is to establish a secure environment where people may confide in one another and find encouragement to face and solve life's problems. Through fellowships, bible studies, mentorship, retreats, and special events, we support men ministering to other men for their spiritual development and encouragement as well as for training and empowering them to serve others in the church community.

Current leaders

  • Benard Akaye - Chairperson
  • Desmond Matete - V.Chairperson

WOMEN

Women week, Second week of May every year

Whatever stage of life you are in, PEFA Kangemi A's women's ministry is open to women of all ages. We offer you a place where you can grow spiritually and become rooted in God's word, find grace, inspiration, and hearts that can identify with you. We support women ministering to other women for their spiritual development and uplift as well as education and empowerment to serve others in the church community through gatherings, bible studies, prayers, retreats, and special events.

Current leaders

  • Agnes Nzula - Chairperson
  • Margaret Njoki - V.Chairperson

YOUTH

Youth Week, First week of April every year

The youth ministry is a setting for young adults to make friends and learn about God's word. We work hard to support our youth in developing closer bonds with God, their peers, and to have a positive impact in the community. There are many voices competing for the attention of young people in this world. We think that the best guidance for what's best for their life can be found in God's word.

Current leaders

  • Charity Mwangangi - Chairperson
  • Boniface Ngugi - V.Chairperson
